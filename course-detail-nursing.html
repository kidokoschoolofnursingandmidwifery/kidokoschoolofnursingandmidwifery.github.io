Certificate in Nursing

Course Information

  • Course Price Shs. 1,850,000
  • Course Duration 2.5 years
  • Course Start July 22, 2024

Description

The Certificate in Nursing program at Kidoko School of Nursing and Midwifery equips aspiring nurses with essential skills and knowledge for a successful healthcare career. Our curriculum combines theoretical foundations with hands-on clinical practice, preparing students to deliver high-quality patient care.

Students gain practical experience through supervised clinical placements in hospitals and healthcare facilities. These real-world experiences are crucial for developing competence and confidence in the nursing profession.

"Kidoko's modern facilities and dedicated faculty ensure that every student receives top-notch training and support."

Our experienced faculty consists of seasoned healthcare professionals committed to student success. They provide personalized mentorship and guidance, fostering a supportive learning environment that promotes academic and practical excellence.

Graduates of the Certificate in Nursing program are well-prepared to pursue licensure and employment in diverse healthcare settings. Our career support services, including job placement assistance and career planning, help students transition smoothly into the nursing workforce. Join Kidoko School of Nursing and Midwifery and start your journey towards a rewarding nursing career.
